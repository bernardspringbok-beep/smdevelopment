About Us

SM Development Centre NPC is a registered non-profit company based in De Aar, Northern Cape. We serve the Pixley Ka Seme District with accessible education support and community development initiatives.

Our core work includes after-school tutoring aligned to the CAPS curriculum, youth empowerment through career guidance and psycho-social support, and adult learning programs such as ABET literacy, ICT training, and learner's licence preparation.

We operate from De Aar with outreach in Britstown and Hanover, partnering with schools, parents, and local stakeholders. Our approach is practical: community-led planning, evidence-based interventions, and measurable outcomes for learners and families.
